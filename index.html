Invasive Plant Identification

Wild Parsnip

Yellow umbrella shaped flowers. Causes severe burns when sap touches skin in sunlight.

Giant Hogweed

Very large plant with white umbrella flowers. Sap can cause severe blistering burns.

Buckthorn

Invasive shrub that spreads aggressively in forests and fields.

Poison Ivy

Leaves of three plant that causes itchy rash when touched.

Dog Strangling Vine

Highly invasive vine that spreads rapidly across rural properties.
